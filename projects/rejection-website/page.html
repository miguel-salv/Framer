Rejection Website

A website to offer gentle support and actionable advice for anyone coping with job rejections.

Software

3 months

CMU

Visit Project

Overview:

This website offers gentle support and actionable advice for CMU students coping with job rejections. Whether its your first rejection, a loss from your dream job, or repeated rejections, you will find resources, tips, and steps to help you move forward.

Features:

  • Pages for first job rejections, dream job rejections, and repeated rejections.

  • Switch between light and dark themes with a toggle.

  • Provides external links and resources for further reading.
